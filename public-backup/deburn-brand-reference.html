Brand Quick Reference

Prohibited: AI-Generated Aesthetics

Deburn must feel human-crafted, not software-generated. The following are strictly forbidden:

Purple in any context
Blue-to-purple gradients
Unnecessary card containers
Glowing effects or lens flares
Generic SaaS dashboard layouts
Decorative borders or shadows

Scandinavian Minimalism

Deburn embraces Nordic design principles—functional beauty, understated elegance, and warm minimalism. Our visual language creates calm through considered simplicity. We actively reject AI-generated aesthetics: no purple gradients, no unnecessary boxes, no generic tech-startup looks.

Less, but better
No purple—ever
Warm neutrality
No unnecessary boxes
Vector icons only
Never use emoji
