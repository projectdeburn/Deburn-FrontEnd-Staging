# Terms of Service

## Overview

The Terms of Service page displays Eve's terms of service agreement. Users must accept these terms during registration. It's a static content page accessible to all users.

## Components

- Eve logo link
- Page title and last updated date
- Content sections:
  - Acceptance of Terms
  - Description of Service
  - User Accounts
  - Acceptable Use
  - Disclaimer
  - Intellectual Property
  - Termination
  - Contact Us
- Back to Eve link

## User Flow

1. User clicks Terms of Service link from Login, Register, or footer
2. Reads terms of service content
3. Scrolls through sections
4. Clicks Back to Eve to return to app
