# Cookie Policy

## Overview

The Cookie Policy page explains how Eve uses cookies and similar technologies. It details cookie types, purposes, and how users can manage their preferences.

## Components

- Eve logo link
- Page title and last updated date
- Content sections:
  - What Are Cookies
  - How We Use Cookies
  - Cookies We Use (table)
  - Managing Cookies
  - Third-Party Cookies
  - Updates to This Policy
  - Contact Us
- Cookie table with Name, Purpose, Duration
- Back to Eve link

## User Flow

1. User clicks Cookie Policy link from footer or settings
2. Reads cookie policy content
3. Reviews cookie table for specific cookies used
4. Learns how to manage cookie preferences
5. Clicks Back to Eve to return to app
